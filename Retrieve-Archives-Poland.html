Expert Polish Archive Retrieval

Finding Hard-to-Reach Historical Documents & Vital Records in Poland

Millions of Polish and Jewish records are not digitized, are written in antiquated languages, or are buried deep within local registry offices (USC) and state archives. If you have hit a brick wall due to language barriers or complex Polish bureaucracy, I act as your boots on the ground in Poland to retrieve your family's documents.

Genealogy & Family History (No Citizenship Required)

You do not need to be applying for a European passport to use my services. Many of my clients are simply researching their family tree and want to hold a piece of their history. If you just want to find your grandfather's birth record, your great-grandparents' marriage certificate, or historical death records for personal genealogical research, I will locate, retrieve, and translate these documents for you.

Documents I Retrieve Beyond Standard Vital Records

As a professional genealogist and legal researcher, I conduct deep-dive research into multiple Polish archival systems to retrieve:

  • Birth, Marriage & Death Certificates: Locating historical civil registry documents across changing borders and jurisdictions.
  • Property & Land Registries (Księgi Wieczyste): Uncovering historical deeds and property ownership records.
  • Court & Probate Records: Retrieving legal heirship documents, historical court rulings, and civil contracts.
  • Military & Conscription Archives: Finding records of ancestors who served in the Polish Army (1918-1939).
  • Holocaust & Ghetto Records: Navigating the Jewish Historical Institute (ŻIH) and concentration camp archives for proof of residence or persecution.
  • Voter & Tax Registries: Finding business licenses and municipal voting lists that prove an ancestor's residency in Poland.

Why My Approach Works

Retrieving documents in Poland requires the ability to read the documents once you find them.

  • Multi-Lingual Expertise: Documents from the same town might be written in Polish, Russian, German, Hebrew, or Yiddish. I speak fluent Polish, English, Yiddish, and Hebrew, allowing me to translate and verify the documents immediately.
  • Legal Background: My background in law means I understand the strict legal requirements the Polish government demands. I don't just find a piece of paper; I ensure it is the correct legal proof you need.
  • Dual Presence: Residing in both Israel and Poland allows me to work directly with Israeli clients while physically accessing archives across Poland.
