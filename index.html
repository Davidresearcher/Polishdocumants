About Polish Heritage

Polish Heritage is dedicated to helping people reconnect with their Polish roots and successfully complete Polish citizenship procedures. I combine local knowledge in Poland with an understanding of what clients abroad need for their legal and genealogical processes.

Whether you are building your family tree or proving your ancestry for citizenship by descent, I focus on clear communication, reliable timelines, and documents that match legal requirements.
