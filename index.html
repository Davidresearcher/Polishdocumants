My Story

I am Abraham Neumann, a professional Polish Jewish genealogist living in Israel. Over the years, I have successfully completed numerous complex cases for clients worldwide, specializing in heir and probate research, vital record retrieval for citizenship purposes, and building extensive family trees for individuals with Polish Jewish ancestry.

While I coordinate and analyze the research from Israel, I have a dedicated, experienced network of professional researchers stationed all over Poland. Upon my direction, my team is ready to physically comb through local registry offices (USC) and state archives to locate and retrieve the exact birth certificates and historical documents you need to prove your heritage.
