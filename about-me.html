Lead Researcher

Abraham Neumann

Specializing in Polish archival research and citizenship law with over 1 year of experience.

My journey began with a personal passion for genealogy, which quickly grew into a professional pursuit. I realized that for many people living abroad, the Polish legal system and archival network can be a maze of bureaucracy.

I founded Polish Heritage to be the bridge between you and your history. My expertise lies not just in finding documents, but in understanding the *stories* behind them and the legal weight they carry for citizenship applications.

  • Expertise: Civil & state Archives, Legal Translation
  • Languages: English, Yiddish, Hebrew
  • Mission: To make Polish heritage accessible to everyone.
